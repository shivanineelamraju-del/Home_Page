ABOUT US

A chapter, not a committee.

ACM-W BPHC is the student chapter of ACM's Council on Women in Computing at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus.

We exist to support, celebrate and grow the community of women in tech on campus — through technical projects, mentorship circles, speaker sessions and events that are open to everyone who believes computing is better when it's built by everyone.

The chapter runs on six domains that keep the wheels turning year-round — each with its own bulletin, task board and progress tracker, so members always know what's shipping next.

See what each domain is working on →
  • Mentorship

    Pairing juniors with seniors across every branch, every semester.

  • Building

    Real projects, hackathon squads, and open-source sprints.

  • Belonging

    A room where asking a "basic" question is never awkward.
